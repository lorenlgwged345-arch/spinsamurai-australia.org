Our Commitment to Responsible Gambling

  • Player Protection: We prioritize the well-being of our players above all else. We strive to protect vulnerable individuals and minors from the risks associated with gambling.
  • Awareness and Education: We aim to raise awareness about responsible gambling practices and the potential risks of excessive gambling. We provide easy access to information and resources.

  • Fair Play: Our Spin Samurai Casino platform operates with integrity, ensuring fair gaming outcomes and transparent practices.

1. Preventing Underage Gambling

It is strictly forbidden for anyone under the age of 18 (or the legal age of majority in your jurisdiction) to open an account or gamble at Spin Samurai Australia . We employ robust age verification procedures to ensure that only eligible individuals can access our services.

  • During the registration process, we require users to confirm they are of legal age.
  • We reserve the right to request proof of age from any customer and may suspend an account until satisfactory verification is received.
  • We encourage parents and guardians to use filtering software (e.g., Net Nanny, CyberPatrol) to prevent minors from accessing gambling websites.

2. Self-Assessment and Awareness

We encourage all players to regularly assess their gambling habits. If you are concerned about your gambling or believe it might be becoming a problem, consider the following questions:

  • Do you spend more money on gambling than you can afford to lose?
  • Do you gamble to escape from problems or feelings of boredom, loneliness, or depression?
  • Have you tried to stop gambling but been unsuccessful?
  • Do you feel guilt or remorse after gambling?
  • Has gambling caused any financial problems for you or your family?
  • Has gambling negatively impacted your relationships, work, or studies?

If you answered yes to several of these questions, it might be advisable to seek help.

3. Tools for Responsible Gambling

Spin Samurai Australia provides several tools to help you manage your gambling effectively:

Deposit Limits

You can set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits on your account. Once a limit is set, you will not be able to deposit more than that amount within the chosen timeframe.

To set a deposit limit, please log in to your account and navigate to the "Responsible Gaming" section or contact our customer support.

Loss Limits

You can set a limit on the amount you can lose over a specific period (daily, weekly, or monthly).

To set a loss limit, please log in to your account and navigate to the "Responsible Gaming" section or contact our customer support.

Wagering Limits

You can set a limit on the total amount you can wager over a specific period.

To set a wagering limit, please log in to your account and navigate to the "Responsible Gaming" section or contact our customer support.

Session Limits

You can set a limit on the duration of your gaming sessions. The system will automatically log you out once the set time is reached.

To set a session limit, please log in to your account and navigate to the "Responsible Gaming" section or contact our customer support.

Cool-Off Period

If you need a short break from gambling, you can set a cool-off period for your account. During this period, you will not be able to deposit or play, but you will be able to log in and withdraw any remaining funds. Cool-off periods can range from 1 day to 6 months.

To activate a cool-off period, please log in to your account and navigate to the "Responsible Gaming" section or contact our customer support.

Self-Exclusion

For a more extended break, you can choose to self-exclude from Spin Samurai Australia . Self-exclusion means your account will be closed for a period of 6 months, 1 year, or permanently. During self-exclusion, you will not be able to log in, deposit, or receive marketing communications.

To activate self-exclusion, please contact our customer support team directly at support@spinsamurai-australia.org. Our team will guide you through the process and ensure your request is handled promptly.

4. External Help and Support

If you feel that your gambling is becoming a problem, or if you are concerned about someone else's gambling, we strongly encourage you to seek professional help. Several organizations offer confidential advice and support:

  • Gambling Help Online (Australia): Provides free, confidential 24/7 support and counselling for problem gamblers and their families. Visit www.gamblinghelponline.org.au or call 1800 858 858.
  • Gamblers Anonymous: A fellowship of men and women who share their experience, strength, and hope with each other that they may solve their common problem and help others to recover from a gambling problem. Visit www.gamblersanonymous.org.
  • Gambling Therapy: Offers online support to anyone seriously affected by gambling. Visit www.gamblingtherapy.org.
  • Lifeline (Australia): Crisis support and suicide prevention services. Call 13 11 14.
